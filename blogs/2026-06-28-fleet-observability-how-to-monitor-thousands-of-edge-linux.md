Netdata examines the challenge of monitoring geographically dispersed Linux endpoints - robots in warehouses, EV chargers across a city, kiosks in retail, IoT gateways in the field - at scale. It argues that traditional datacenter-focused monitoring tools fall short once rare failures become daily occurrences across thousands of distributed devices.
