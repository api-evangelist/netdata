Understand why tracking cumulative resource consumption is crucial for accurate process monitoring. Accurate Process Monitoring with Netdata: Why Tracking Cumulative Resource Consumption Matters Tracking the cumulative resource consumption of processes, including short-lived and exited children, is a rare feature in monitoring tools – and it’s one of the standout capabilities Netdata offers. Most mainstream solutions, like Datadog’s process monitoring and Prometheus’s Node Exporter, focus on active processes and only collect metrics per PID.
